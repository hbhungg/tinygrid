Forecasting

Energy demand for all 6 buildings are forecasted. The forecasted energy demand spands from 1st October 2020 to 30th November 2020 which is given in a 15 minute time steps. Every unit of energy is measured in kW. This is calculated using the historical 15-minutely
energy demand of 6 buildings from Monash University, Melbourne, Australia. We neeed the predicted energy demand to get the amount of energy that might be needed to optimally schedule the battery and activity.
Solar production for all 6 solar panels are forecasted. Power production is forecasted from 1st October 2020 to 30th November 2020 which is given in a 15 minute time steps. Every unit of power is measured in kW. This is calculated using the historical 15-minutely
energy demand of 6 buildings from Monash University, Melbourne, Australia. The predicted power production is used to get the amount of energy that is produced in order to get the optimal battery and activity schedule.
